Mores Pay for Field Agent Spending Control

Reimbursements, direct payment orders and QRIS — submitted from the field, approved by Finance, disbursed by the gateway, and reconciled on a double-entry ledger.

Roles: Agent · Finance · Super Admin · Auditor (read-only)

What's inside

◫

Agent mobile app

Snap a receipt, submit a reimbursement or vendor payment order, pay merchants by QRIS with a 6-digit PIN — all against a monthly quota.

✓

Approval workflow

Finance reviews the queue with the receipt on screen, approves or rejects with a note. Approval triggers the disbursement automatically.

⇄

Gateway disbursement

Payouts move through a clearing account and settle asynchronously via signed webhooks — Xendit-style, mocked for the sandbox.

▤

Double-entry ledger

Balances are never stored, always derived. Every movement writes balanced debit/credit rows you can export for reconciliation.

◔

Limits & quotas

Per-transaction caps and monthly quotas per agent, enforced server-side before anything reaches Finance.

≡

Reports & audit

Usage per account, category breakdowns, CSV exports for accounting, and an append-only audit trail of every decision.

Why we built it

Growth gave you more companies. It also gave you more places for the truth to hide.

Most groups don't struggle because their people are slow. They struggle because the work of seeing the whole picture still happens by hand — one workbook per company, merged late at night, trusted with fingers crossed.

We started MORES HV inside a group with exactly that problem. Five operating companies, one board, and a month-end that swallowed a week before anyone could say what the group had actually earned. So we built the tool we wished we had: books stay per company, the consolidated view assembles itself, and intercompany transactions cancel out on their own instead of quietly inflating the total.

The result isn't just faster reporting. It's the confidence to walk into a board meeting, or a deal, already knowing your number is right.

What it does

Everything the finance core of a group needs, and little it doesn't.

We kept the scope honest: the finance work a multi-company group does every month, done well — not a sprawling ERP you'll spend six months switching on.

Consolidation

One group, seen as one

Keep a clean book per company and read the whole group from a single dashboard. Intercompany lines are eliminated automatically, so the number you present is the number that's real.

Bank reconciliation

From a BCA receipt to a booked entry

Paste a transfer receipt or drop in a mutasi-rekening file. MORES HV reads it, flags duplicates by reference, and books it to the right account in a couple of clicks — the part of month-end that used to eat days.

Project profitability

Know which work actually paid

See revenue, cost, profit and margin for every project, over time and side by side. The quarterly guess becomes a number you can defend.

Budgets

Plans you check more than once

Set the budget per account and per company, then watch realization against it — variance and percent used, current the moment an entry is posted.

Statements & export

The reports your auditors expect

P&L, balance sheet, trial balance and cash flow, with charts. Export anything to Excel or PDF; import journals, chart of accounts and budgets from templates your team already knows.

Access & trust

The right people, the right view

Admin, Accountant and read-only Viewer/Auditor roles, down to which companies each person can see. Balanced double-entry and source-tracked journals mean the trail is always there when someone asks.

Who it's for

Different seats at the table. The same clarity.

Owners & boards

"I need the group's real number before tomorrow's meeting."

Open the dashboard and it's already there — every posted entry, consolidated, with intercompany taken out. No overnight merge, no asterisks.

Finance & accounting

"Month-end shouldn't cost us a week."
